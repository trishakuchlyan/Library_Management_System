General Notice & Guidelines

  • Students must present their institutional identity card during book collection at the library counter.
  • A student is permitted to borrow a maximum of 2 books simultaneously.
  • Maximum borrow period is 14 days, after which late fee fines of ₹2.00/day will apply automatically.
  • All members must abide by the library's rules and agreements.
