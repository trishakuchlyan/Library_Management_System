General Library Guidelines

  1. Library Card: Library card is non-transferable. The borrower is responsible for any transactions done under their card.
  2. Book Limit: General students are entitled to borrow at most 2 books simultaneously. Honors/Research students may borrow up to 3 books.
  3. Borrow Period: Textbooks can be issued for a maximum of 14 days. Reference manuals and rare journals cannot be taken outside the reading room.
  4. Renewal Policy: Re-issue/renewal of a book is allowed only once, provided there is no pending reservation requisition from another member.
  5. Late Return Fine: A fine of ₹2.00 per day will be levied automatically starting from the 15th day of loan. Remittance must be paid at the admin desk to unlock borrowing privileges.
  6. Book Damage/Loss: If a book is mutilated, damaged, or lost, the student must replace the book with its latest edition or pay twice the current market value of the book.

Contract & Legal Agreement

DECLARATION & INDEMNITY CONTRACT:

I hereby solemnly declare that I shall abide by all the Rules and Regulations of the Michael Madhusudan Memorial College Central Library. I understand that the library resources are public property funded by the Government. Any attempt to damage, steal, write upon, or mutilate any text constitutes a disciplinary violation.

I agree to return books within the designated timeline and settle all fine liabilities immediately. The library administration reserves full authority to withhold my university Admit Card, semester results, and character certificate if library dues are not settled before final exams.

By registering on this portal, I digitally sign this indemnity contract and agree to be bound by the university library tribunal rules.
